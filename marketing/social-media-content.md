## Content Pillars

### 1. Google Ads Tips (40%)
Educational content about Google Ads best practices.

**Post Templates:**
- "3 keywords that are draining your ad budget right now 💸"
- "The #1 mistake small businesses make with Google Ads"
- "Your Google Ads checklist for [Season/Event]"
- "[Industry] owners: here's what your competitors are doing with Google Ads"

### 2. Product Showcases (25%)
Demonstrate AdMaster Pro features in action.

**Post Templates:**
- "Watch our AI create 5 ads in 12 seconds ⚡"
- "I just said 'find money leaks' and saved $400/month"
- "Voice command demo: managing Google Ads hands-free"
- "New feature: [Feature name] — here's what it does"

### 3. Customer Stories (20%)
Social proof and testimonials.

**Post Templates:**
- "[Business] went from wasting $2K/month to a 3.2x ROAS with AdMaster Pro"
- "Before AdMaster Pro vs. After — see the difference"
- "Why [Business Owner] switched from their $3K/month agency"
- Customer quote graphic with stats

### 4. Behind the Scenes (15%)
Company culture, product development, team insights.

**Post Templates:**
- "Here's what we shipped this week at AdMaster Pro"
- "Why we built [Feature] — the story behind it"
- "Our AI just got smarter. Here's how."
