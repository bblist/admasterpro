# AdMaster Pro — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary: Small Business Owners

### Demographics
- **Business size:** 1-50 employees
- **Revenue:** $100K–$10M annually
- **Industries:** Service businesses (plumbing, dental, legal, HVAC, auto repair, landscaping), local retail, restaurants, professional services
- **Location:** English-speaking markets (US, UK, Canada, Australia)
- **Decision maker:** Owner, owner-operator, or office manager
- **Age range:** 30-60

### Psychographics
- Time-poor — wearing multiple hats (sales, operations, marketing)
- Know they need online advertising but find Google Ads confusing
- Have been burned before by agencies or DIY attempts
- Value simplicity and results over features
- Prefer talking/texting over clicking through complex dashboards

### Pain Points
1. **Wasted ad spend** — "I'm spending $2K/month on Google Ads and don't know what I'm getting"
2. **Complexity** — "Google Ads is too complicated. I don't have time to learn it"
3. **Agency costs** — "My agency charges $1,500/month and I can't tell if they're doing anything"
4. **Lack of visibility** — "I don't know which ads are working and which are wasting money"
5. **Time** — "I need to run my business, not manage ad campaigns"

### Buying Triggers
- Just started Google Ads and feeling overwhelmed
- Received a large Google Ads bill with poor results
- Agency contract is ending and looking for alternatives
- Competitor is showing up above them in Google
- Seasonal push (back to school, holiday, spring)

### Objections
- "I've tried AI tools before and they didn't work"
- "Will the AI understand my specific business?"
- "What if it runs bad ads?"
- "I'm already spending too much, I can't add another expense"

### Objection Responses
- "Nothing goes live without your explicit approval — you're always in control"
- "Upload your business details to the Knowledge Base and the AI learns your industry, pricing, and unique selling points"
- "Start with our free plan — 10 AI messages, no credit card. See results first"
- "Most clients save 20-40% on wasted spend, which more than covers the subscription"

## Secondary: Marketing Agencies

### Demographics
- **Agency size:** 2-20 employees
- **Clients managed:** 5-50 active accounts
- **Services:** PPC management, digital marketing, local SEO
- **Price point:** Pro plan ($149/mo)

### Pain Points
1. Too much time on manual campaign management
2. Hard to scale — each new client adds proportional workload
3. Client onboarding takes too long
4. Difficulty showing ROI to clients

### Value Proposition
- Manage all clients from one dashboard
- AI does the repetitive work — frees up strategists
- Branded audit reports for new business pitches
- Per-seat cost makes multi-account management profitable

## Anti-Personas (Who We DON'T Target)

- **Enterprise brands** with $100K+/month ad spend (need custom solutions)
- **Marketing professionals** who want granular manual control
- **Businesses not using Google Ads** — we optimize Google Ads specifically
- **Free-only users** with no intent to upgrade (serve but don't pursue)

## Customer Journey

### Awareness → Interest
- Google search: "Google Ads management tool," "AI ads platform," "save money on Google Ads"
- Free audit landing page → AI-powered website analysis → email capture
- Social media content about Google Ads tips and wasted spend

### Interest → Evaluation
- Free plan signup → 10 AI messages → see draft quality
- Pricing page comparison → Starter vs Pro
- FAQ page → builds confidence ("nothing goes live without approval")

### Evaluation → Purchase
- Upgrade CTA in dashboard ("You've used 8/10 free messages")
- Email drip: case studies, tips, feature highlights
- Free audit report → "Want to fix these issues? Start with AdMaster Pro"

### Purchase → Retention
- Onboarding wizard → connect Google Ads → upload business info
- First week: daily tips from AI ("Did you know you can say 'Find money leaks'?")
- Monthly usage reports showing value delivered
