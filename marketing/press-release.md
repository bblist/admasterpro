## NobleBlocks LLC Launches AdMaster Pro — AI-Powered Google Ads Management Platform for Small Businesses

*New SaaS tool uses GPT-4o and Claude to help small business owners create better Google Ads, detect wasted spend, and grow revenue — without needing a marketing degree*

**New York, NY — February 2026** — NobleBlocks LLC today announced the general availability of **AdMaster Pro**, an AI-powered Google Ads management platform built specifically for small businesses and marketing agencies. The platform combines OpenAI's GPT-4o and Anthropic's Claude to automate ad creation, detect budget waste, and deliver actionable campaign optimization — all through a conversational interface that anyone can use.

### The Problem

Small businesses spend an estimated $150 billion on Google Ads annually, yet studies show that up to 76% of that spend is wasted on poorly targeted keywords, weak ad copy, and campaigns that run without oversight. Most small business owners lack the time, expertise, or budget to hire a dedicated PPC specialist or agency, leaving them to navigate Google's increasingly complex advertising platform alone.

### The Solution

AdMaster Pro acts as an always-available AI advertising assistant. Users connect their Google Ads account, and the platform immediately begins analyzing campaign performance, identifying money leaks, and generating optimized ad copy tailored to their specific business.

**Key features include:**

- **AI Ad Copy Generation** — Create high-converting text, display, and responsive search ads using GPT-4o or Claude, trained on the user's own business context via a built-in Knowledge Base
- **Money Leak Detection** — Automatically identifies wasted spend on low-performing keywords, poor ad placements, and budget inefficiencies
- **Voice & Text Commands** — Manage campaigns through natural conversation — ask questions, request changes, and get insights by simply typing or speaking
- **Free Website Audit Tool** — Any business can get a comprehensive AI-powered website audit at no cost, covering SEO, performance, accessibility, and content quality
- **Multi-Account Management** — Agency-ready: manage multiple Google Ads accounts from a single dashboard (Pro plan)
- **Call Tracking** — Monitor phone call conversions directly within the platform

### Pricing

AdMaster Pro is available in three tiers:

| Plan | Price | Highlights |
|------|-------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0/month | 10 AI messages, 1 campaign |
| **Starter** | $49/month | 200 AI messages, 5 campaigns, display ads, call tracking |
| **Pro** | $149/month | Unlimited AI messages, unlimited campaigns, multi-account, priority support |

All paid plans include a 7-day free trial. No credit card required for the Free plan.

### What Sets AdMaster Pro Apart

Unlike enterprise tools such as Optmyzr ($249+/month) or agency services ($1,500+/month), AdMaster Pro is priced and designed for the small business owner who manages their own marketing. The platform replaces the complexity of Google Ads with a simple conversational interface — users describe what they want, and the AI handles the technical execution.

"Small business owners shouldn't need a marketing degree to run effective Google Ads," said the AdMaster Pro team. "We built AdMaster Pro so that a plumber, a dentist, or a local bakery owner can get the same quality of ad management that used to require a $5,000-per-month agency."

### Technology

AdMaster Pro is built on Next.js 14 with a PostgreSQL database, deployed on AWS. The platform integrates directly with the Google Ads API and uses Stripe for secure payment processing. All AI interactions use API-tier access to OpenAI and Anthropic, meaning user data is never used to train third-party models.
